About me

What can I say about me?

The first time I found a solution to a problem in my code, the first time I created a website, or when I learned a new technology, I have felt the same way. A rush of adrenaline invaded my body, my heart going to a fast pace, and my mind desperately searching for the explanation of how I could have done such things. At that moment I understood the true meaning of the word "passion". I am passionate about web development; I am passionate about learning new things every day and applying them in my creations. Being in front of a computer while programming, I feel that everything I can imagine, I can make it come true. This is where I belong. I'm Leo. Nice to meet you!
